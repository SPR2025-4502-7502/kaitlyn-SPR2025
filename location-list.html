Best 7 Record Shops in Kansas City

These are the top 7 record store locations in Kansas City, MO. These spots have a great overall atmosphere for those looking for a fun day of shopping around KC, or for the determined collector. They offer a wide selection of music for everyone!

Mills Record Company

4045 Broadway Blvd,
Kansas City, MO
64111
(816) 960-3775

Mills Record Company is a record store located in the heart of Kansas City. They offer a great collection of new and used records, cds, cassettes, books and more. Known for their welcoming atmosphere and wide selection of music,, Mills is a great spot to browse new releases, favorite classics, rare finds, or catch a live show from local artists!

FM Music Vintage Sounds

206 E 18th Ave,
North Kansas City, Mo
64116
(816) 716-2770

FM Music Vintage Sounds is a locally owned record shop located in North Kansas City. FM Music offers a cozy atmosphere, densely packed with an impressive selection of used and new vinyl, cassettes, CDs, and local music releases. This shop is a goldmine for retro collectors and fans of obscure finds.
